Beer Pieterjan Prakken

Beer Prakken is a PhD candidate in philosophy at the University of Groningen, Guest Researcher at Utrecht University and Predoctoral Fellow at Center for Cultural Sociology at Yale University. researching the intersection of play, humor, and politics. His interdisciplinary work examines rhetorical and ethical dimensions of humor and play in politics and culture. He holds bachelor's and dual master's degrees in history and philosophy. In addition, Beer's historical research investigates urban spaces and the lives of Black washerwomen in Antebellum New Orleans. Beer has appeared in prominent Dutch media outlets such as Volkskrant and Trouw.
